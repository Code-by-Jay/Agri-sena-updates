ABOUT AGRISENA

Welcome to Agrisena, your trusted companion in agriculture. Our mission is to empower farmers with essential information, innovative tools, and timely updates to help them make informed decisions and thrive in their agricultural endeavors.

Agrisena provides a wide range of services tailored to the needs of farmers, including:

  • Weather Warnings: Stay ahead of unpredictable weather patterns with real-time alerts for storms, cyclones, and floods.
  • Market Prices: Access up-to-date crop prices to maximize your profits and make better market decisions.
  • Disaster Alerts: Receive early warnings for natural disasters to safeguard your crops, livestock, and livelihood.
  • Agricultural Guidance: Learn best practices, crop tips, and modern farming techniques to improve yields and efficiency.
  • 7-Days weather forecast: Access the current location of device /manually selected your city it gives 7 days forecast output

At Agrisena, we are committed to building a sustainable and prosperous future for our farmers. With technology and innovation at the heart of our platform, we strive to address the challenges faced by the agricultural community.

Join us on this journey and take a step toward revolutionizing the way you approach farming. Together, we can cultivate success!
